Gauss Sphere is cutted at sigma_range*sigma 
Gauss Sphere = weight * exp ( - ((x^2 / sigmax^2)+(y^2 / sigmay^2)+(z^2 / sigmaz^2)) 
mrcImageFileSize is (sigma_range*sigmax*2) x (sigma_range*sigmay*2) x (sigma_range*sigmaz*2)
